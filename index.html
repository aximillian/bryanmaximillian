About

I am a final-year Informatics student at Atma Jaya Yogyakarta University, pursuing a career as a developer. I am seeking opportunities to apply my skills and contribute to impactful projects within your organization. My academic experience has honed my abilities in web development, mobile development (Android and cross-platform), and API integration.

I am a highly motivated and responsible individual with proven communication skills and a strong desire to learn. I excel in both team and individual settings and am good at building collaborative relationships.

My project portfolio demonstrates my practical skills:

- Online Book Order Website: I developed a full-stack CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) website for online book ordering, demonstrating my proficiency in front-end and back-end web development.

- Cross-Platform Marketplace App: Using Flutter, I built a multi-platform marketplace application, showcasing my ability to develop for both Android and iOS from a single codebase.

- Android Development Training (Dicoding): I completed Dicoding's Android Developer learning path up to the Intermediate level, solidifying my understanding of Android development principles and best practices.

- Beauty Center Integration Project: My most recent project involves integrating a mobile Android application with a website for a beauty center, demonstrating my skills in API integration and cross-platform communication.
